## Biography
Christoph Hess, known as Cr7z, is a German rapper born in Sangerhausen in the former East Germany. He fled with his single mother to Rosenheim in Bavaria via Hungary and Austria in the final months of the DDR. Hess began rapping in German at a young age, inspired by Hip-Hop. He started releasing music on platforms like MySpace, SoundCloud, and YouTube as Chriz MC. His first album, "Pein" (Pain), was released digitally in 2008. In 2010, he signed with 58Muzik under rapper Absztrakkt. He has released multiple albums, including collaborations, and founded his own label Arjuna in 2017 with DJ Eule. Cr7z has over 300 free tracks on YouTube and has toured Germany. He emphasizes multiculturalism and Hip-Hop culture in his work.

Cr7z distanced himself from Nazism, addressing his family history in songs like "Schwarze Flügel" from "Pein," where he raps about his name "Heß" and the Nazi symbols sprayed on his house wall. He affirms commitment to multicultural youth culture.

## Relationship with Rudolf Heß
Cr7z (Christoph Hess) is a grand-nephew of Rudolf Heß, the Deputy Führer of Nazi Germany and close associate of Adolf Hitler. Rudolf Heß was born in 1894 in Alexandria, Egypt, and became a high-ranking Nazi official. He attempted a solo peace mission to Britain in 1941, was captured, and imprisoned for life at Spandau Prison until his suicide in 1987. Christoph Hess, born in 1984, is related through his grandfather, Johann Fritz Hess, who took over the family import company Hess & Co. in Alexandria.

This familial connection has been a point of controversy for Cr7z. In his lyrics, he acknowledges the burden of the name and the historical association, using it to reflect on Germany's past. He explicitly rejects Nazism and uses his platform to promote anti-racist messages.
