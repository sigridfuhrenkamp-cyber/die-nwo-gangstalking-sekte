## Abstract
Analysis of **information disorder** on social media using NLP, topic modeling, and knowledge graphs. Research questions: (1) distribution of misinformation, disinformation, malinformation across platforms; (2) recurring themes and visibility; (3) role of **AI** as authoritative and/or spreader agent; (4) strategies to combat information disorder. AI is both a tool for fact-checking and truthiness bots and a potential **amplifier of false narratives**. Strategies include digital literacy and critical thinking. Definitions: **Misinformation** = incorrect information without intent to deceive; **Disinformation** = intentional false/manipulated information to harm/manipulate; **Malinformation** = real information distorted to harm or manipulate judgement.
